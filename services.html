Frequently Asked Questions

What should I bring to a yoga class?

Bring a yoga mat, water bottle, and comfortable clothing. We provide props like blocks and straps.

Do I need to book in advance?

Booking is recommended, especially for popular classes. You can reserve a spot online.

Are classes suitable for beginners?

Yes, we offer beginner-friendly classes and modifications for all levels. Our instructors are skilled at providing variations for all abilities.

Do you offer private yoga sessions?

Yes, we offer personalized one-on-one sessions tailored to your specific goals and needs. Contact us to learn more.
