About Our Journey

Discover the philosophy and passionate heart behind your serene outdoor yoga experience in Gaborone, Botswana.

Our Philosophy: Harmonizing with Nature

At Yoga Outdoor Studio, we deeply believe in the transformative power of connecting yoga with nature. Practicing under Gaborone's open skies isn't just about physical postures; it's about enhancing mindfulness, fostering profound tranquility, and truly aligning your body, mind, and spirit with the natural world around you.

Our Story: A Vision Rooted in Botswana

Founded in 2020, Yoga Outdoor Studio was born from a heartfelt vision to seamlessly integrate the ancient practice of yoga with the breathtaking natural landscapes of Gaborone. Our founders, inspired by Botswana's unique beauty and serene environment, meticulously created a sanctuary where holistic wellness and the raw splendor of nature converge, offering an unparalleled yoga experience.

Meet Our Exceptional Instructors
