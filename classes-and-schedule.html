Class & Booking FAQs

Do I need prior yoga experience?

Not at all! Many of our classes are designed for all levels, and instructors provide modifications for beginners.

What should I bring to class?

A yoga mat, water bottle, and comfortable athletic wear. We have mats available for rent if you don't have one.

How do I book a class?

You can book a class directly from this page by clicking the "Book Now" button next to your desired class, or visit our Book a Class page.

Can I cancel or reschedule a class?

Yes, please notify us at least 24 hours in advance if you need to cancel or reschedule your booking to avoid charges.
