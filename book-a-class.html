Frequently Asked Questions

How do I pay for my classes?

We accept mobile payments (e.g., Orange Money, MyZaka), bank cards, and cash directly at our Gaborone studio for your convenience.

Can I change my class booking?

Yes, you can! Please contact us at least 24 hours before your scheduled session if you need to switch class types or reschedule, subject to availability.

What should I bring to class?

We recommend bringing your own yoga mat, a water bottle, and comfortable clothing. Don't worry if you don't have a mat; we provide mats for rent if needed.

Are classes suitable for beginners?

Absolutely! Our classes are designed to be inclusive, with modifications offered for all levels. Beginners are always welcome and encouraged!
